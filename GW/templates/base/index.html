Is Global Warming a Real Issue?

Global Warming is a very important issue that can have a long-term impact on human civilization. Scientist observed that Earth's climate has rapidly increased due to human acitivies such as burning fossil fuels and human over-consumption. As a result, Earth's atmosphere resulted in increase green house gas levels which then raised Earth's average surface temperature. It will cause rising sea levels, and more extreme weather conditions.
